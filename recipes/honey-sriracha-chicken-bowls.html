Honey Sriracha Chicken Bowls

A sticky, spicy and sweet way to cook your chicken thighs.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Chicken Thighs
  • 150ml Sriracha
  • 50g Honey
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 100g Spring Onions
  • 6 cloves Garlic
  • 300g Steamed Rice

Steps

  1. Place the chicken skin-side down in a frying pan and cook on a low-medium heat for 10 mins until the skin is really crispy.
  2. Remove the chicken, leaving the chicken fat behind and chop the chicken in to small bites.
  3. Combine the sriracha, honey and soy sauce in a bowl.
  4. Finely chop the spring onions and the garlic.
  5. Save some spring onions and then add the other spring onions and the garlic to the chicken fat.
  6. Cook for 5 mins with a pinch of salt until softened and fragrant.
  7. Pour in the sauce and your chicken pieces and bring to a bubble until the sauce is thick and the chicken is cooked through.
  8. Serve in bowls and sprinkle over the saved spring onions. Serve with steamed rice and enjoy.
