Lobio (Georgian Bean Salad)

A traditional Georgian kidney bean salad

Typically served at room temperature or chilled, the dish is defined by its use of dark-red kidney beans cooked until soft but firm, and is almost always paired with walnuts, fresh herbs, and a bold blend of spices like cinnamon, cloves, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 or 1 whole jalapeño pepper, minced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, finely chopped
  • 3 Tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 x 15oz tin of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Whisk together the first seven ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Stir in the kidney beans and season to taste
  3. Chill the salad for 1 hour in the fridge so the flavours combine
  4. Serve chilled or at room temperature
