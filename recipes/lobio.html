Lobio (Georgian Bean Salad)

A traditional Georgian kidney bean salad

Lobio is a beloved Georgian kidney bean salad (or stew) traditionally made with dark-red kidney beans and a bold walnut paste seasoned with fresh herbs and spices.

While the word "lobio" simply means "beans" in Georgian, it most commonly refers to this specific dish served at room temperature or cold as a starter.

Ingredients

  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/2 or 1 whole jalapeño pepper, minced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 80g walnuts, finely chopped
  • 3 Tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 x 400g tin of kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Whisk together the first seven ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Stir in the kidney beans and season to taste
  3. Chill the salad for 1 hour in the fridge so the flavours combine
  4. Serve chilled or at room temperature
