Rigatoni con Salsiccia

A delicious tomato and sausage pasta with a key ingredient of fennel seeds.

Ingredients

  • 400g Sausage Meat
  • 1tbsp Fennel Seeds
  • 400g Rigatoni
  • 4 cloves Garlic, chopped
  • 1sp Chilli Flakes
  • 400g Passata
  • 150ml White Wine
  • 50g Pecorino

Steps

  1. Tear up the sausage meat into bite-sized pieces and add them into a pan with a drizzle of olive oil and cook for 5 minutes until they begin to brown then remove.
  2. To the pan, add the garlic, fennel seeds, chilli flakes, some olive oil and a sprinkle of salt.
  3. Cook on a low heat for a minute or so (don’t let the garlic brown).
  4. Add the wine to deglaze the bottom of the pan and bubble it down. Then add the sausage meat back in.
  5. Start cooking the rigatoni as per the packet instructions.
  6. Pour in the passata and season with salt. You need to bubble it down until it’s thicker.
  7. After the pasta has cooked for 5 mins, add a few tablespoons of pasta water to your sauce.
  8. When the pasta is ready, stir it in to the sauce.
  9. Serve it into your bowls and top with the pecorino. Enjoy!
